Iliana Fili

Welcome to my world! I'm Iliana Fili, a 22-year-old psychologist hailing from the beautiful country of Greece. Psychology isn't just a field of study for me; it's a passion that fuels my curiosity about the human mind and behavior.

When I'm not immersed in textbooks and lectures, you'll find me surrounded by my two adorable cats, Fioroula and Sky. Their playful antics and unconditional love bring so much joy and warmth to my life.

Writing is my sanctuary. I pour my thoughts and reflections into articles that explore the intricacies of life. From the mundane to the profound, I love delving into the essence of existence and sharing my insights with others.

Join me on this journey of self-discovery and exploration as I navigate the realms of psychology, cherish the companionship of my furry friends, and weave stories about the beauty of life itself. Together, let's uncover the wonders of the human psyche and embrace the magic of being alive.
